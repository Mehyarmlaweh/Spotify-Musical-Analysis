Usage
=====

Home Page
---------

The **Home Page** provides an overview of the application and its features. Click the "Access the Analysis Dashboard" button to explore the data.

Dashboard
---------

The **Dashboard** includes the following sections:

1. **Average Popularity by Genre**: A bar chart showing the average popularity of music genres.
2. **Audio Features by Genre**: Histograms showing the distribution of audio features (e.g., danceability, energy) for a selected genre.
3. **Correlation Between Popularity and Audio Features**: A heatmap showing the correlation between popularity and audio features.
4. **Top 10 Most Popular Tracks**: A table listing the most popular tracks.

Each section includes a button to get insights from Claude LLM based on the data.
